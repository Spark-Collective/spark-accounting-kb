# Annual accounts filing (NBB)

A BV files its annual accounts electronically at the **NBB Central Balance Sheet Office** each year. The model depends on the company's [[company-size-criteria|size]].

## The rule (verify_live)

- **Deadline:** within **30 days of approval** by the general meeting, and at the latest **7 months after the financial-year close**. For a calendar-year 2025, the deadline is **1 August 2026**. [search]
- **Electronic only:** filed via the NBB application (no counters since 2010). [search]
- **Model by size:** **micro model** (micro companies, minimal notes), **abridged/verkort model** (small companies), **full model** (larger). [search]
- **Format: XBRL preferred** (cheaper, structured); PDF possible (and sometimes mandatory for specific sectors). [search]

### Filing fees 2026 (excl. penalties) [search]

| Model | XBRL | PDF |
|---|---|---|
| Micro | EUR 67.00 | EUR 137.30 |
| Abridged | EUR 89.40 | EUR 159.50 |
| Full | EUR 379.50 | EUR 449.70 |

- **Late filing penalty** (from the 8th month after close), micro/abridged: ~EUR 151 in the 9th month, ~EUR 227 in months 10-12. [search]

> Fees, deadlines, and penalties change yearly. **Confirm with the [NBB](https://www.nbb.be/en/central-balance-sheet-office)** before filing.

## Why it matters

This is a hard annual deadline with an automatic penalty , a scheduled agent task: assemble the micro/abridged model from the closed books, file in XBRL, pay the fee, and record proof.
