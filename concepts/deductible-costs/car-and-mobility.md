# Deductibility of car and mobility costs

One of the most-asked practical questions for a management BV. The deductible fraction of car costs in Belgium is **not flat** , it depends on the vehicle (CO2/fuel type), and the rules have been tightening (phase-out of fossil-fuel deductibility, favourable treatment for electric).

> **verify_live + confidence: medium.** Car deductibility changes frequently and by vehicle. **Always confirm the current formula and percentages with [FOD Financiën](https://financien.belgium.be)** (or the accountant) before booking , do not apply a remembered percentage.

## The shape of the rule (verify the numbers)

- **Deductible % is formula-based** on the vehicle's CO2 emissions and fuel type; a fossil car is well below 100%, an electric car has been treated more favourably, with a transition regime over several years.
- **Fuel/energy costs** follow their own deductible fraction.
- A **benefit in kind (VAA)** arises if the company car is used privately , see [[payroll-director]] (planned), and it interacts with the deduction.
- VAT on a car is typically only partly deductible (capped, with a professional-use limit).

## Booking

Car costs hit class 6 expense accounts; the non-deductible fraction is a tax add-back at year-end ([[year-end]], planned), not a bookkeeping exclusion , book the full cost, disallow the right fraction in the corporate-tax computation.
