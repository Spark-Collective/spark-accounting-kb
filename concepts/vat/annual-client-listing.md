# Annual client listing (klantenlisting)

A yearly VAT obligation: a list of the **Belgian VAT-registered customers** to whom the company supplied goods/services in the previous calendar year **for more than EUR 250 (excl. VAT)**, with the annual turnover per customer.

## The rule (verify_live)

- **Deadline: 31 March** of the following year, via **Intervat**. [fod]
- **Threshold: EUR 250** (excl. VAT) of annual turnover per Belgian-VAT customer , below that, the customer is not listed. [fod]
- **Nil listing** if there are no such customers (e.g. all customers are foreign or each under EUR 250). [fod]
- **Franchise (small enterprise):** a business under the [[vat-rates-and-regimes|exemption regime]] reports its **total turnover** via the listing. [fod]
- **Mixed/partial taxpayers** file the listing but exclude operations exempt under art. 44 of the VAT code. [fod]
- **Ceasing activity:** file within **3 months** of losing VAT-payer status. [fod]

> Confirm the current threshold and deadline with [FOD Financiën](https://financien.belgium.be).

## Why it matters

A fixed annual deadline the agent prepares from the year's sales ledger , distinct from the cross-border [[ec-sales-list]]. Runs alongside the [[periodic-vat-return-intervat|periodic VAT return]].
