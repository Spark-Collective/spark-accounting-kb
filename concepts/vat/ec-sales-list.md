# EC sales list (intracommunautaire opgave)

The periodic listing of **intracommunity transactions** with VAT-payers in other EU countries , the cross-border counterpart of the domestic [[annual-client-listing]]. Filed via **Intervat**.

## The rule (verify_live)

- **Deadline: the 20th** of the month following the period (quarter or month). [fod]
- **Frequency follows the VAT return:** quarterly filers may file quarterly, **but must switch to monthly** if exempt IC supplies of goods + triangular sales exceed **EUR 50,000** in the current or any of the previous four quarters. [fod]
- **What it reports:** IC supplies of goods (code **L**), triangular transactions (code **T**), and services taxed where the customer is established (code **S**). [fod]
- **Electronic** via Intervat for periodic-return filers. [fod]

> Confirm the current threshold, codes, and deadline with [FOD Financiën](https://financien.belgium.be).

## Why it matters

Driven by the [[intracommunity-and-medecontractant|reverse-charge]] sales the company books , the agent compiles it from the same 0%-VAT cross-border invoices, alongside the [[periodic-vat-return-intervat|VAT return]].
