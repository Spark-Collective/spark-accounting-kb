# Per diem for domestic business travel (forfaitaire dagvergoeding)

A fixed daily allowance the company pays the director to cover meals and drinks on a professional trip **within Belgium**. Done right, it is a clean optimization: **tax-free for the director** and **100% deductible for the company**.

## The rule (verify_live)

- **Daily allowance:** a fixed amount per qualifying travel day. [astro]
- **Monthly allowance:** allowed for regular travel, on condition of **on average 16 travel workdays per month**. [astro]
- **Tax-free for the director** if all conditions are met; **100% deductible** for the company. [astro]
- **Maximum amounts are indexed and updated periodically** (new amounts applied from 01.03.2026, previous set 01.03.2025-28.02.2026). [astro]

> The maximum amounts change (roughly twice a year). **Confirm the current ceiling with [FOD Financiën](https://financien.belgium.be)** before paying; exceeding it makes the excess taxable.

## Why it matters

Unlike a [[restaurant-and-meals|restaurant cost]] (~69%, no VAT recovery), a compliant per diem is fully deductible and untaxed , a better-structured way to cover travel meals. The agent tracks qualifying travel days and applies the current ceiling, as part of [[director-remuneration]].
