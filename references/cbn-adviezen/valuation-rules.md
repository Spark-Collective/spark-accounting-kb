# CBN advies , valuation rules (waarderingsregels)

A `references/cbn-adviezen/` page: the CBN/CNC principles for valuing items in the annual accounts , the foundation under [[annual-accounts-nbb|the annual accounts]] and [[double-entry-basics|the books]].

## What the advies says

- **Going concern (continuity):** valuation assumes the company continues its activity. The governing body must **annually reassess** whether that assumption still holds; material uncertainty must be disclosed, and if continuity no longer applies, **adjusted valuation rules** are used (CBN-advies 2018/18). [cbn]
- **Acquisition value + individualization:** each asset is valued **separately at acquisition value**; inventory/asset cost is individualized unless another stated method (weighted average, FIFO) is used (CBN-advies 132/7). [cbn]
- **Consistency (bestendigheid):** valuation rules are applied **consistently year to year**; a change must be disclosed and justified in the notes. [cbn]
- **Prudence (voorzichtigheid):** depreciation, impairments, and [[provisions]] meet prudence, sincerity, and good faith; a provision is formed for a very probable cost even if the probability is below 50.1%. [cbn]

## Grounds

[[annual-accounts-nbb]] and the year-end judgements; sits above [[depreciation]] and [[provisions]] as the common valuation frame.
