# CBN / WVV , size criteria (art. 1:24 / 1:25)

A `references/cbn-adviezen/` page: the **legal source** of the size thresholds the practice page [[company-size-criteria]] applies. Defined in the WVV itself; the CBN advises on their application (consolidated basis, the two-year rule).

## What the law says (verify_live)

- **Small company (art. 1:24 WVV):** at the balance-sheet date of the last closed year, exceeds **no more than one** of , avg **50** employees, turnover excl. VAT **EUR 11,250,000** (raised from 9,000,000), balance-sheet total **EUR 6,000,000** (raised from 4,500,000). [search]
- **Micro company (art. 1:25 WVV):** a subcategory of small that is **not a parent or subsidiary** and exceeds no more than one of , avg **10** employees, turnover **EUR 900,000** (raised from 700,000), balance-sheet total **EUR 450,000** (raised from 350,000). [search]
- Assessed on a **consolidated basis** where relevant, and on the **two-year consistency** rule (a threshold crossing only bites if exceeded in two consecutive years).

> The figures are indexed/raised periodically. Confirm the current thresholds (and the consolidated-basis rule) , see [[company-size-criteria]] (`verify_live`) and the NBB.
