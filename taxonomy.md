# Taxonomy , controlled tags

Every tag used in a page's frontmatter must appear here. Need a new tag? Add it here first (with a one-line meaning), then use it. This prevents tag sprawl and keeps the graph navigable.

## Domains
- `company-forms` , legal forms, incorporation.
- `bookkeeping` , double-entry, chart of accounts, journals, retention.
- `vat` , BTW: registration, rates, regimes, returns, listings, e-invoicing.
- `deductible-costs` , aftrekbare kosten and their limits.
- `corporate-tax` , vennootschapsbelasting, advance payments.
- `payroll-director` , director pay, social contributions, benefits in kind.
- `year-end` , annual accounts, depreciation, provisions, closing.
- `workflows` , the agent's standard operating procedures.

## Qualifiers
- `bv` , applies to a vennootschap (BV/SRL).
- `eenmanszaak` , applies to a sole trader / natural person.
- `intervat` , relates to the Intervat filing channel.
- `peppol` , relates to e-invoicing / Peppol.
- `verify-live` , page contains a changeable fact to re-check at use time (mirrors the `verify_live` frontmatter flag).
- `belgium` , Belgian-specific (default for this KB; use sparingly).
